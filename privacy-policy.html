Overview

WingSync is designed to help riders coordinate during active group rides. This policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and how users can request deletion.

Location collection during active rides

WingSync collects precise location information only during active rides when ride coordination features are being used. Location may continue updating while the app is minimized or the screen is locked during an active ride so ride coordination can continue.

Location is shared only with riders who are participating in the same active ride. Location sharing stops when the rider exits the ride or when the leader ends the ride.

No hidden tracking

WingSync does not use hidden location tracking. WingSync does not track location after a ride ends, does not publicly broadcast rider location, and does not share active ride location outside the same ride group.

Information that may be collected or stored

WingSync may collect or store information needed to operate ride coordination features, including rider name, Google account email if sign-in is used, Firebase user ID, local rider/device/session ID, ride ID, active ride membership, leader/rider state, speed, heading, precise location during active rides, heartbeat or status information, timestamps such as joinedAt, updatedAt, and heartbeatAt, telemetry state, and app preferences such as metric unit.

How information is used

Information is used to operate active ride coordination, show squad status, help riders see ride context, maintain app reliability, and support safety-first ride coordination.

Sharing

During an active ride, riders in the same ride may see rider name, location, speed, status, and telemetry needed for coordination. Google account email is not described as shown to other riders. WingSync does not sell rider location data.

Ads

WingSync may show small banner ads outside active ride screens. Ads are not shown on the active runtime map screen. Google Mobile Ads or Google services may process ad-related data if ads are enabled.

Service providers

WingSync may use Firebase, Google services, or similar infrastructure providers to process data for app operation. Firebase and Google services may process authentication, database, cloud infrastructure, security, diagnostics, advertising-related data where applicable, and reliability data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Data retention

Active ride telemetry is used for realtime coordination and session continuity. When a ride ends, active ride documents and active rider telemetry records may be deleted. Limited operational records may be retained temporarily for security, abuse prevention, diagnostics, infrastructure reliability, legal compliance, support, or service provider behavior. WingSync does not promise permanent instant deletion of every record.

User deletion requests

Users can request deletion by emailing support@ridewingsync.com. Users may also have an in-app Delete Account option. Account deletion may be blocked while a user is actively leading a ride; the user should transfer leadership or end the ride first. See Delete Account for more information.

Deletion requests may include ride data, rider name, telemetry, account data if available, and related stored information that can reasonably be associated with the user.

Children's privacy

WingSync is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information to WingSync, contact support so the request can be reviewed.

Safety and device limits

Location accuracy, GPS availability, network connectivity, and battery settings may affect WingSync behavior. Riders should not rely on the app as their only source of safety information.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated as WingSync changes. The effective date will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions or help with deletion requests, contact support@ridewingsync.com.
