Data deletion

Delete Your WingSync Account and Data

WingSync users can request deletion from the app if available or by contacting support.

In-app option

If available, open WingSync, go to Settings, tap Delete Account, and confirm. If you are leading an active ride, deletion may be blocked until you transfer leadership or end the ride.

Request by email

Email support@ridewingsync.com with the subject: WingSync Account Deletion Request.

Include the Google account email used for WingSync, approximate date of last use, and confirmation that deletion is requested.

What data may be deleted

Depending on what information exists in WingSync systems, a deletion request may include WingSync user profile or account data where available, rider name, account identifiers, active ride membership records, ride data associated with the user where available, telemetry or location records associated with active or retained ride data where available, local session state only when deletion is performed in-app, and account data if available through the Firebase/Auth deletion flow.

Verification

WingSync may need to verify that the requester is authorized to delete the data before processing the request.

What may be retained

Limited records may be retained temporarily for security, abuse prevention, diagnostics, infrastructure reliability, legal compliance, dispute handling, backups, or service provider logs before removal through normal retention processes.

Processing

We will review and process deletion requests within a reasonable period after verifying the request. WingSync does not promise instant deletion or automatic deletion from all systems unless that behavior is implemented and available.

Contact

For privacy questions or help with deletion requests, contact support@ridewingsync.com.
