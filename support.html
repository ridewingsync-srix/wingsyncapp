Battery and GPS reliability guidance

WingSync depends on device GPS, network access, and battery availability. If ride visibility is unreliable, review these checks before the ride begins:

  • Keep location permission enabled during active rides.
  • Keep an internet connection available while participating in a ride.
  • Keep notifications enabled if used for active ride status.
  • Some phones may pause GPS or background updates when battery optimization is enabled.
  • For best reliability, users may choose to allow unrestricted battery or background activity for WingSync. This is optional and remains under user control.
  • Keep the device charged before longer rides.
  • Use a safe, legal stop before changing app or device settings.

GPS accuracy can be affected by tunnels, dense buildings, weather, device hardware, network coverage, or operating system restrictions.

Users can stop active ride sharing by exiting the ride or ending the ride if they are the leader.
